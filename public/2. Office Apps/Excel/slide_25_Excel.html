Excel - Find Personnel with Specific Schedule

Description: This prompt will help you find personnel with a specific schedule. Identifying personnel with specific schedules can assist in planning and ensuring adequate coverage during different time slots.

Prompt: "Find all personnel with the schedule '08:00 - 16:00’.”

Outcome: The spreadsheet will display only the personnel with the schedule '08:00 - 16:00', making it easier to manage and allocate resources for this time slot.
