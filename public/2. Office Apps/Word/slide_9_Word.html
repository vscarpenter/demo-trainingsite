Word - Identifying Visual Aids for Data Representation

Description: This prompt suggests areas in the report that would benefit from visuals, such as charts, tables, or diagrams, to clarify complex information.

Prompt: "Identify sections where visuals would enhance comprehension. Suggest charts, tables, or diagrams for each applicable section, specifying the data they should represent. Explain why each visual is necessary and how it improves understanding of the content."

Outcome: Outcome: A list of recommended visuals that improve data presentation and comprehension.
