Copilot for Outlook

Copilot in Outlook helps you triage email, draft and refine replies, summarize long threads, and turn conversations into tasks and calendar actions—without leaving the inbox.

Great for

  • Summarizing long, multi‑party threads and extracting action items
  • Drafting replies in your voice with the right tone and length
  • Turning emails into tasks, follow‑ups, or calendar events
  • Quickly catching up after PTO or meetings with ‘catch‑up’ summaries
Example prompt: “Summarize this thread in 5 bullets with owners and due dates, then draft a concise reply confirming my next steps and asking for missing details.”
Tips: State audience and tone (e.g., executive, friendly, direct). Ask Copilot to list assumptions before sending. Use Refine to shorten or add bullet points.

Common scenarios

  • Inbox triage at the start of day with summaries and suggested replies
  • Drafting externally facing emails with a compliance‑friendly tone
  • Scheduling a follow‑up meeting directly from a thread
High‑level overview prepared for quick onboarding.
