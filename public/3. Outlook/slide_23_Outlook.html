Consolidating Meeting Schedules and Recurrence Patterns

Description: This prompt helps create an organized schedule of all meetings, including details on one-time and recurring sessions.

Prompt: List all meetings with their respective dates, start and end times, and recurrence patterns (if applicable). For each meeting, specify the department or individuals responsible for attending and note any conflicting schedules that may need adjustment.

Outcome: A consolidated schedule that provides clear visibility of all meetings, helping avoid conflicts and ensuring proper attendance.
