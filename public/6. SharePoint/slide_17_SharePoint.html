This document, issued by the Naval Inspector General, outlines the policy for posting inspection, investigation, and special study reports on the NAVINSGEN public reading room website. It includes criteria for determining which reports should be posted, redaction procedures to protect sensitive information, and intended outcomes such as compliance with FOIA requirements and potential reduction in FOIA requests.
